## I.4 PROHIBITED WITHOUT APPROVAL

The following are **strictly prohibited** without explicit written approval:

### Confidential Information
- Disclosure of any Client Confidential Information
- Proprietary technical details
- Business strategies or plans
- Internal processes or procedures

### Metrics and Data
- Client usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Adoption data
- Financial information
- User counts

### Client Content
- Screenshots of Client's products
- Client's documentation or materials
- Code samples from Client's systems

### Endorsements
- Implying Client endorses Provider
- Testimonials without written consent
- Award or recognition claims involving Client

## I.5 APPROVAL PROCESS

### Requesting Approval

1. **Submit Request:** Provider sends written request to Client contact
2. **Include Materials:** Attach proposed marketing content
3. **Specify Use:** Describe intended use and channels
4. **Response Time:** Client responds within 10 business days

### Approval Form

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Date | Request date |
| Requested Use | Specific marketing use |
| Materials | Attached content |
| Channels | Where content will appear |
| Duration | How long content will be used |

### Approval Response

Client shall respond with:
- **Approved:** As submitted
- **Approved with Changes:** With specified modifications
- **Not Approved:** With reason (optional)
- **No Response:** After 10 business days, request is deemed not approved

### Duration of Approval
Unless otherwise specified, approvals are:
- Valid for 12 months from approval date
- For the specific use case requested
- Revocable with 30 days written notice

## I.6 LOGO AND TRADEMARK USAGE

### If Approved

If Client approves logo/trademark usage:

**Required:**
- Use only approved versions of logos
- Maintain minimum clear space
- Use proper trademark attribution (™ or ®)
- Follow Client's brand guidelines

**Prohibited:**
- Modifying or distorting logos
- Using outdated versions
- Placing on busy backgrounds
- Implying partnership beyond service relationship

### Brand Guidelines
Upon approval, Client will provide:
- Approved logo files
- Brand usage guidelines
- Required attribution text

## I.7 PRESS RELEASES

### Joint Press Releases
Any press release mentioning both parties requires:
- Mutual written approval of content
- Approval of distribution timing
- Approval of distribution channels

### Unilateral Press Releases
Provider shall not issue press releases:
- Announcing the engagement without approval
- Referencing Client name without approval
- Containing Client Confidential Information

### Client's Rights
Client may issue press releases regarding the engagement, provided:
- Content is factually accurate
- Provider's Confidential Information is not disclosed
- Provider receives courtesy copy before release

---

## I.8 SOCIAL MEDIA

### Provider's Social Media

**Permitted without approval:**
- General capability posts (no Client identification)
- Industry commentary unrelated to Client
- Sharing Client's public content (without endorsement implications)

**Requires approval:**
- Posts identifying Client
- Posts about the engagement
- Tagging Client accounts
- Sharing project outcomes

### Interaction Guidelines
Provider shall not:
- Comment on Client's public posts implying relationship (without approval)
- Respond to Client mentions in ways disclosing engagement
- Engage in social media disputes involving Client

## I.10 AWARDS AND RECOGNITION

### Award Submissions
Provider may submit the engagement for industry awards, provided:
- Client is notified in advance
- Submission does not disclose Confidential Information

- Client success attributable solely to Provider
