## 5. FEES AND PAYMENT

### 5.5 Late Fees

**[CHANGED]**

~~Invoices not paid within five (5) calendar days of the due date shall accrue interest at **0% per month**.~~

Invoices not paid within five (5) calendar days of the due date shall accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (18% annually) on the unpaid balance, or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

### 5.6 Invoice Disputes

**[NEW SECTION]**

Client must notify Provider in writing of any invoice dispute within **five (5) business days** of invoice receipt. The notice must specify the disputed amount and basis for dispute. **Client shall pay all undisputed amounts by the due date** regardless of any pending dispute.

### 5.7 Collection Costs

**[NEW SECTION]**

In the event Provider must undertake collection efforts for overdue amounts, Client shall reimburse Provider for reasonable collection costs and attorneys' fees to the extent permitted by applicable law.

### 5.8 Suspension for Non-Payment

**[ENHANCED - Previously simple suspension clause]**

~~Intent Solutions reserves the right to suspend all services and withhold future deliverables if any undisputed invoice remains unpaid for more than 10 days past its due date.~~

(a) **Notice.** If any undisputed invoice remains unpaid for more than ten (10) calendar days past its due date, Provider may deliver written notice of intent to suspend Services.

(b) **Cure Period.** **[NEW]** Client shall have three (3) business days from receipt of notice to cure the payment default.

(c) **Suspension.** If Client fails to cure, Provider may immediately suspend all Services and withhold all Deliverables until payment is received.

(d) **Timeline Extension.** **[NEW]** The project timeline shall automatically extend day-for-day for the duration of any suspension.

(e) **Remobilization Fee.** **[NEW]** If suspension exceeds ten (10) business days, Client shall pay a remobilization fee of **$2,500** before Services resume to compensate for context-switching and rescheduling.

### 5.9 License Conditioned on Payment

**[NEW SECTION]**

The license grants under Section 6 become effective only upon receipt of full payment for the applicable Deliverables. Until full payment is received, Provider retains all rights in the Deliverables.

**[NEW SECTION]**

If required by law to disclose Confidential Information, the receiving party shall:
- Provide prompt written notice to the disclosing party (to the extent permitted)

## 12. NON-SOLICITATION

**[ENTIRE SECTION IS NEW]**

**12.1 Non-Solicitation of Personnel.** During the term of this Agreement and for **twelve (12) months** thereafter, neither party shall directly solicit for employment any personnel of the other party who were involved in performing or receiving the Services.
