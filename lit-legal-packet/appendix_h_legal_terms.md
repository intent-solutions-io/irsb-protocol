## H.4 EXPORT CONTROL

### Compliance
Both parties shall comply with applicable export control laws and regulations, including U.S. Export Administration Regulations (EAR) and sanctions administered by OFAC.

### No Restricted Parties
Each party represents that it is not:
- Listed on any U.S. government restricted party list
- Located in, or a national of, a sanctioned country
- Acting on behalf of any restricted party

### Export of Deliverables
Client is responsible for compliance with export laws when distributing Deliverables internationally.

## H.5 ANTI-CORRUPTION

### FCPA Compliance
Each party shall comply with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and similar anti-corruption laws.

### No Improper Payments
Neither party shall make or offer:
- Bribes to government officials
- Facilitation payments
- Improper payments to any person to secure business advantage

### Disclosure
Each party shall promptly disclose any potential violation of this Section H.5.

---

## H.6 RECORDS AND AUDIT

### Record Keeping
Each party shall maintain accurate records relating to this Agreement for three (3) years following completion.

### Audit Rights
Either party may, upon reasonable notice (not less than 10 business days), audit the other party's relevant records to verify compliance with this Agreement. Audits shall:
- Be conducted during normal business hours
- Not unreasonably disrupt operations
- Be at the auditing party's expense (unless material breach found)

---

## H.7 SUBCONTRACTING

### Provider's Right to Subcontract
Provider may use subcontractors to perform portions of the Services, provided:
- Provider remains responsible for subcontractor performance
- Subcontractors are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement
- Provider does not subcontract the entirety of the Services

### Client Notification
Upon Client's request, Provider will identify any subcontractors used for the Services.

### No Assignment of Obligations
Subcontracting does not relieve Provider of any obligations under this Agreement.

## H.8 RELATIONSHIP OF PARTIES

### Independent Contractors
The parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates:
- Employment relationship
- Partnership or joint venture
- Agency relationship
- Fiduciary duty

### No Authority to Bind
Neither party has authority to:
- Bind the other party to any agreement
- Make representations on behalf of the other party
- Incur obligations for the other party

### Taxes and Benefits
Each party is responsible for its own:
- Income taxes
- Employment taxes
- Employee benefits
- Workers' compensation

## H.9 EQUITABLE RELIEF

### Availability
Either party may seek equitable relief (including injunction and specific performance) for:
- Breach of confidentiality obligations
- Infringement of intellectual property rights
- Violation of non-solicitation provisions

### No Bond Required
Neither party shall be required to post a bond as a condition of obtaining equitable relief.

### Not Exclusive
Equitable relief is in addition to, not in lieu of, other remedies available at law.
